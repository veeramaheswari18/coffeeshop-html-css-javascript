what are you method payment?

For orders made online, we accept payments through: • Debit/Credit card • AllEasy • Lyka Gems; and • GCash *Note: Cash on Delivery could be available depending on which delivery channel you ordered through. You can check our FAQs page for the complete list of our delivery partners

where is coffee project locate?

Coffee Project currently has 56 outlets around the Metro Manila, Calabarzon and in few provincial locations. You can find us at the following locations: • Alabang Level 1 • Alabang Level 4 • Antipolo • Bulacan • Butuan • Cabanatuan • Cauayan • CDO • Cebu • Crosswinds • Dasmariñas • Edsa Shaw • Evia • Evia Black • España • Gapan • General Trias • General Santos
